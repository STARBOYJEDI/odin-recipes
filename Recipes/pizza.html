Pizza

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pepperoni
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella

How to make it

  1. Roll out the pre-made pizza dough into a nice big circle.
  2. Cover with tomato sauce.
  3. Sprinkle over with some mozzarella.
  4. Place sliced pepperoni on top.
  5. Bake in the oven for 20 minutes.
