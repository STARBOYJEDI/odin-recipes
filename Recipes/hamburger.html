Hamburger

Ingredients

  • Sesame seed bun
  • Ground beef
  • Egg
  • Mustard
  • Onion & garlic
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Tomato
  • Cheese
  • Red Onion

How to make it

  1. First, lightly oil your grill and preheat it to medium temp.
  2. Combine whisked egg with diced onion, garlic, salt & pepper.
  3. Add the ground beef and mix well using your hands or a fork.
  4. Press the seasoned ground beef into patties.
  5. Spray the grill with cooking spray before placing your burgers on it, then close the lid to cook.
  6. Cook them for 6-8 minutes per side until they reach 70Â°C in the center.

Assemble:

  1. Toast the buns on each side.
  2. Assemble the other ingredients starting with lettuce, tomato, burger, cheese and red onion on top.
